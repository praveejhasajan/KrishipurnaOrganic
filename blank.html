Fox Nut (Makhana)

Scientific Name: Euryale ferox
Common Names: Makhana, Fox Nut, Lotus Seed

Fox Nut, also known as Makhana, is a highly nutritious and healthy snack, widely consumed in India and around the world. It is rich in protein, fiber, and essential minerals like magnesium, potassium, and phosphorus. Makhana is low in calories and fat, making it an ideal snack for weight management and heart health.

  • 100% natural and organic
  • Gluten-free and vegan
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Supports heart health and digestion
  • Ideal for snacking, cooking, and desserts
