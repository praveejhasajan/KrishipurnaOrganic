About Krishipurna Organic Pvt. Ltd.

Krishipurna Organic Pvt. Ltd. is dedicated to bringing the purity of Indian agriculture to global markets through sustainably grown and ethically sourced food products.

  • Specializing in premium Makhana, Millets, Chuda, Sattu, and other traditional Indian food grains.
  • Committed to organic farming practices and global food safety standards.
  • Trusted export partner delivering quality with traceability, certifications, and customized packaging.

With roots in the fertile lands of Bihar, we aim to empower farmers and promote Indian superfoods globally. Our infrastructure, supply chain, and customer-first approach make us a reliable choice for health-focused buyers and distributors worldwide.

1. What organic products does Krishipurna Organic Pvt. Ltd. offer?

We offer a range of premium organic products including Makhana (Foxnuts), Millets, Sattu, Jaggery, Chuda, and other traditional Indian superfoods sourced from sustainable farms in India.

2. How does Krishipurna ensure product quality and organic certification?

All our products undergo strict quality control and comply with national and international organic certification standards including USDA Organic, India Organic, and FSSAI certifications to ensure purity and safety.

3. Do you provide export services globally?

Yes, Krishipurna Organic Pvt. Ltd. exports its organic food products worldwide, including countries in the USA, UAE, Australia, Europe, and other key international markets.

4. What makes Krishipurna's Makhana (Foxnuts) superior in quality?

Our Makhana is handpicked from the best water bodies in Bihar and processed using traditional methods ensuring high nutritional value, crispiness, and zero chemical usage, making it one of the finest organic Foxnuts in the market.
