Organic Poha (Chura)

Scientific Name: Oryza sativa (Flattened Rice)
Common Names: Poha, Chura, Flattened Rice, Beaten Rice

Organic Poha (Chura) is a traditional Indian breakfast staple made from flattened rice grains. It is light, easy to digest, and packed with essential nutrients. Our Poha is processed from premium quality organic rice, ensuring purity and natural taste. It is ideal for quick snacks, breakfast recipes, and healthy meals.

  • 100% natural and organic
  • Gluten-free and vegan
  • Rich in iron, carbohydrates, and dietary fiber
  • Quick to cook and easy to digest
  • Perfect for breakfast, snacks, and traditional Indian dishes
