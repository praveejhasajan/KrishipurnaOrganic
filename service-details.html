  • Farm-level organic cultivation under expert supervision with certification support.
  • International-grade cleaning, sorting, grading, and moisture-controlled packaging units.
  • Customized private labeling and co-packing support for startups and brands globally.
  • Full-stack export documentation (FSSAI, phytosanitary, invoice, BRC standards on request).
  • Contract farming for international clients ensuring stable supply chains and traceability.

  • Freight forwarding, cold chain handling, and international shipping logistics.
  • Custom export sourcing including niche organic pulses, jaggery, spices, and flour blends.
